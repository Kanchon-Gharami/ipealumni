How to Register

  • Sign In into your account, for new member click Sign Up
  • Update your profile
  • Make payments (Click here to explore payment process) for reunion using Bank Account, Bkash, Rocket or Nagad
  • Goto reunion page (Events > Upcoming Event > 1st Reunion)
  • Click on Registration for 1st Reunion
  • Give necessary information and upload payment slip (also keep a copy for yourself)
  • After admin approval (for payment confirmation) successful registration you will get a confirmation mail

Accommodation

Rajshahi offers a rich spectrum of accommodation options to cater to your varied needs, whether you're looking for luxury or affordability. For those seeking a premium stay, the city features three 4-star hotels: Grand River View, Hotel X, and Hotel Royal Raj, each providing luxurious rooms, top-notch amenities, and excellent service. Apart from these luxury options, Rajshahi is home to a variety of mid-range hotels and guesthouses that offer comfortable lodgings and basic amenities. For budget-conscious visitors, there are numerous smaller hotels and hostels scattered across the city. You can also explore short-term apartment rentals or opt for home-stay experiences to get a feel of local culture. With this diverse range of options, you're sure to find something that fits your preferences and budget.
