Vice-Chancellor Message

Dr. Engr. Md. Jahangir Alam

Vice-Chancellor Message

I am pleased to welcome you, as a distinguished alumnus/ alumna of the Industrial & Production Engineering (IPE) Department at RUET. The IPE Department at RUET has a rich legacy of academic excellence and a proud tradition of producing distinguished professionals. We deeply value your contributions and accomplishments, and we are committed to strengthening the bond between our alma mater and each one of you. I envision a future where the IPE Alumni Community is a vibrant and engaged network of professionals who are passionate about supporting each other and the department. A community where we can learn from each other, share our expertise, and collaborate on new and innovative initiatives. Looking forward to your enthusiastic participation and the opportunity to reconnect with you.

Dr. Md. Mosharraf Hossain

Chief Patron Message

Dr. Md. Mosharraf Hossain

The IPE Department at RUET has a long and distinguished history, and our alumni are a testament to the quality education and values instilled by the department. However, the world is changing rapidly, and so must need our alumni community. This website is designed to foster stronger connections between the IPE Department and its cherished alumni, and to provide a platform for networking, knowledge sharing, and collaboration. To ensure you receive updates and engage with fellow alumni, please ensure your contact information is up to date in our alumni database. Your involvement and continued association with RUET IPE Department will undoubtedly contribute to the continued success and prestige of our beloved institution.
